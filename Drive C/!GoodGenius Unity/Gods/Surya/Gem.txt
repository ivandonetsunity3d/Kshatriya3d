Ruby is the astrological, Jyotish gem related to the Sun, which rules one's person confidence, authority, power and status. The Sun is related to the soul and has a great deal to do with career and the person's worldly activities. Success in maintaining a healthy body, vitality, immune system and the willpower of achieve greatness in society is under the purview of the Sun.
Wearing a ruby of at least 1 carat is said to increase popularity and professional status. It will cure diseases of the eyes and of the heart. Ruby can be substituted with pink sapphire (in women), red garnet and red spinel.
